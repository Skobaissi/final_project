The Music

So many choices! Making a decision on how to accompany your big day can seem daunting. We're here to help. Check out the songs we know below (we've included YouTube links of others playing them in case you can't remember how they sound!) We've also included a sample performance.

The classics:

  1. Wedding March (Mendelssohn)
  2. Ave Maria (Schubert)
  3. Canon in D (Pachelbel)
  4. Jesu, Joy of Man's Desiring (Bach)
  5. Meditation (Massenet)


Some less traditional stuff:

  1. Hallelujah (Leonard Cohen)
  2. The Luckiest (Ben Folds)
  3. The Prayer (Celine Dion)
  4. Something (The Beatles)
  5. Blackhawks Theme Song

Our weekly thoughts on music


February 3, 2016

"And those who were seen dancing were thought to be insane by those who could not hear the music."

Friedrich Nietzsche

Words to live by! Dance down your ceremony aisle (if only in your head?) to tunes that change your wedding game. Select songs that mean something to you as a couple and will be part of this memory for years to come.
