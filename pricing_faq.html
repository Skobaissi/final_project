Pricing & FAQs


What does your pricing look like? Our standard rate for ceremony music is $300. We are happy to sit down with you and discuss your budget and if your needs might differ from the below. This package includes:
  • One-hour music consultation
  • 30-minute participation in rehearsal
  • Set-up at wedding ceremony (~45 min-1 hour beforehand)
  • Wedding performance (~30 min-1 hour). This is flexible - we are available to play while guests are entering (up to 15 minutes before the ceremony). We would then play during parent arrival, bridesmaid arrival, bride arrival, and the bride/groom departure
Do you offer anything beyond the ceremony music? Yes, we are happy to play during rehearsal cocktail receptions / dinners, during wedding cocktail receptions, or during another celebration. Cocktail reception playing (for 1 hour) can be added to the above wedding package for $200 (a total package cost of $500).
What music do you play? We play the standard classic wedding hits (e.g., bridal march), but are certainly willing to switch it up with some more contemporary hits (think "The Luckiest" by Ben Folds), or even learn a song that is special to you. See the music section for more details!
How many weddings have you done? We are just starting out! At this point, we've only performed in a few weddings of friends and family. Check out our music section for YouTube samples of our performances. We'd love to add your wedding to the reference list!
