Let's play together




A budget-friendly violin + guitar duo

We do it for the love of music (we have day jobs too), and we believe you deserve great music at an affordable rate. Our standard-rate ceremony music is $300 - add cocktail or rehearsal dinner music for a total of $500.

Something old and something new

We'd like to meet you to understand your wedding vision, your music loves, and how we can create the best possible music experience for your special day. We are flexible and happy to switch it up to learn both new and classic tunes you might enjoy.

Chicago + surrounding suburbs

As Chicago natives, we serve the city as well as surrounding suburbs (up to 1 hour drive from the city).

Welcome! We are Tim and Sara (or "Rhythm of Rose," a product of Tim's last name), and we believe that music has the power to inspire love and infuse any celebration with joy. As a recently engaged couple, we understand that finding the perfect music for your wedding ceremony may not only be stressful (where do you start?) but also expensive. We work full-time and, after playing music together for the past few years, have decided to offer our violin + guitar musical stylings to weddings and other celebrations in the Chicagoland area.


About Tim

After becoming a piano and sax pro at a young age, Tim realized that the guitar offered much more success with the ladies. He taught himself to play in highschool and has been crooning to Mumford and Sons and The Avett Brothers ever since. He even proposed to Sara by guitar and song!

About Sara

Sara was taught by Suzuki Strings starting at age 4 and traveled with the company throughout her childhood. In highschool she decided to pursue other sports, but always knew she would come back to her violin. After settling in to life in Chicago in her 20s, she began taking private lessons and playing with an adult ensemble. She loves weddings and knew she wanted to share wedding music after having the chance to play in her friend's ceremony.
